# Products / 商品介紹

The instruments TXO Options Lab can analyze, with the exact contract facts the
app uses. Every number here is read from `products.js` (frontend) and mirrored in
`server/main.py` (the IB proxy) — change it in one place and this doc is the map.

本文件介紹 TXO Options Lab 支援的商品,以及 app 實際使用的合約規格。所有數字都來自
`products.js`(前端)並對應 `server/main.py`(IB proxy)。

> **Pricing / 定價**
> TXO is priced with **Black-Scholes on spot** (`model: 'bs'`). The seven
> futures options are priced with **Black-76 on the futures price**
> (`model: 'b76'`) — the same `bsPrice` / `bsGreeks` functions with one flag,
> not a second code path. Real futures options are American-style; the app uses
> the European Black-76 value as a fast, close approximation.
> TXO 用**現貨 Black-Scholes**;七個期貨選擇權用**期貨 Black-76**(同一組函式加一個
> 參數)。真實期貨選擇權是美式,app 用歐式 Black-76 當快速近似。

> **Colors / 顏色**: calls are **red**, puts are **teal** — Taiwan T-quote
> convention, applied app-wide (including the US products). 買權紅、賣權綠,全站一致。

## TXO — TAIEX Options / 台指選擇權

**EN.** European, cash-settled options on the TAIEX (Taiwan Capitalization
Weighted Stock Index), listed on TAIFEX. Each point is worth NT$50; strikes step
by 50 points. Expiries are weekly (Wednesday) plus the monthly (third Wednesday,
the gold-dot settlement). IV skews to **puts** — downside hedging demand, typical
of an equity index. Default view: spot 21,850, ATM IV 24%. Not wired to IB in
this app; quotes are generated by the Black-Scholes mock chain.

**中文.** 台指選擇權,標的是加權股價指數,在台灣期交所掛牌,歐式、現金結算。一點 NT$50,
履約價間距 50 點。到期日有週選(週三)和月選(第三個週三,金點結算)。IV 偏向**賣權**
(避險買盤),是股價指數的典型型態。預設 spot 21,850、ATM IV 24%。此 app 不接 IB,
台指用 Black-Scholes mock 報價。

- Multiplier / 乘數: ×NT$50 per index point · Strike step 履約間距: 50
- Fees / 費用(可調): NT$20 per side + 0.1% transaction tax on premium notional

## ZC / ZS / ZW — CBOT Grains / 芝加哥穀物

**EN.** American-style options on CBOT grain futures — Corn, Soybeans, Wheat.
Each contract is 5,000 bushels and quotes in **cents per bushel**, so 1¢ = US$50
(mirroring TXO's ×50, which is why the app reuses the same multiplier). Prices
tick in **eighths of a cent** — the chain shows three decimals like `462.875`.
Strike steps: ZC/ZW 10¢, ZS 20¢. Grain IV smiles skew to **calls** — upside
(drought / weather) risk — the opposite of TXO's put skew. Settlement ~13:20 CT.

**中文.** 芝加哥期交所的穀物期貨選擇權(玉米/黃豆/小麥),美式。一口 5,000 英斗,
報價**美分/英斗**,所以 1¢ = US$50(跟台指 ×50 對稱,app 沿用同一乘數)。跳動單位是
**⅛ 美分**,鏈上顯示三位小數(如 462.875)。履約間距:ZC/ZW 10¢、ZS 20¢。穀物 IV
偏向**買權**(旱災/天氣的上檔風險),跟台指的賣權偏斜相反。約 13:20 CT 結算。

- ZC Corn: default spot 450¢, IV 26%, strike step 10¢
- ZS Soybeans: default spot 1,050¢, IV 20%, strike step 20¢
- ZW Wheat: default spot 550¢, IV 30%, strike step 10¢
- Fees / 費用(可調): ~US$2.5 per side (IB commission + exchange fees), no premium tax
- IB trading classes: `OZC` / `OZS` / `OZW` (standard monthly serials)

## ES — S&P 500 E-mini / 小型標普500

**EN.** Options on the CME E-mini S&P 500 futures — the most liquid equity-index
future in the world. ×US$50 per index point, strikes step by 25. Like TXO it
skews to **puts** (index downside hedging). Default spot 6,120, IV 15%,
settlement ~15:00 CT.

**中文.** CME 小型標普500 期貨選擇權,全球最活躍的股指期貨。一點 ×US$50,履約間距 25。
跟台指一樣偏**賣權**(股指下檔避險)。預設 spot 6,120、IV 15%,約 15:00 CT 結算。

- Multiplier 乘數: ×US$50 / pt · Strike step: 25 · IB class `ES`

## GC — Gold / 黃金

**EN.** Options on COMEX Gold futures, 100 troy ounces per contract, ×US$100 per
$1 move in the gold price. Strikes step by 25. Modeled with a **call** skew.
Default spot US$3,352, IV 18%, settlement ~12:30 CT.

**中文.** COMEX 黃金期貨選擇權,一口 100 金衡盎司,金價每動 US$1 → ×US$100。履約間距 25,
以**買權**偏斜建模。預設 spot US$3,352、IV 18%,約 12:30 CT 結算。

- Multiplier 乘數: ×US$100 / oz · Strike step: 25 · IB class `OG`

## CL — WTI Crude Oil / 西德州原油

**EN.** Options on NYMEX WTI Crude futures, 1,000 barrels per contract, ×US$1,000
per $1 move. Strikes step by $1.00. High IV (energy) and a **call** skew. Default
spot US$72.30, IV 33%, settlement ~14:30 CT.

**中文.** NYMEX 西德州原油期貨選擇權,一口 1,000 桶,油價每動 US$1 → ×US$1,000。履約間距
US$1.00。能源品波動高、偏**買權**。預設 spot US$72.30、IV 33%,約 14:30 CT 結算。

- Multiplier 乘數: ×US$1,000 / $1 · Strike step: 1.00 · IB class `LO`

## NG — Natural Gas / 天然氣

**EN.** Options on NYMEX Henry Hub Natural Gas futures, 10,000 MMBtu per
contract, ×US$10,000 per $1 move. **Fractional strikes** — step 0.10, and the
chain shows two-decimal strikes. Very high IV. Default spot US$3.42, IV 45%,
settlement ~14:30 CT.

**中文.** NYMEX 亨利港天然氣期貨選擇權,一口 10,000 MMBtu,每動 US$1 → ×US$10,000。
**小數履約價** —— 間距 0.10,鏈上顯示兩位小數。波動非常高。預設 spot US$3.42、IV 45%,
約 14:30 CT 結算。

- Multiplier 乘數: ×US$10,000 / $1 · Strike step: 0.10 · IB class `ON`

## Conventions that apply to every product / 通用慣例

- **Points vs currency / 點數與金額.** The chain and pricer work in the product's
  quote unit (points / cents / dollars); P&L in currency = points × multiplier.
- **Fees / 費用.** Each product carries a tunable `fees: { perSide, taxRate }`.
  The "P&L now" and "P&L what-if" cards are **net of estimated round-trip fees**;
  charts stay gross. Adjust per your broker in `products.js`.
- **Per-leg expiry / 各腿到期日.** Every leg can sit on its own expiry, so
  calendars and diagonals work; "at expiry" metrics evaluate at the front expiry.
- **Adding a product / 新增商品.** One entry in `products.js` plus one row in
  `server/main.py` `PRODUCTS` — nothing else changes. See `CLAUDE.md`.

For how to connect real IB data (read-only, research-only), see `server/README.md`.
真實 IB 資料的接法(唯讀、純研究)請見 `server/README.md`。
